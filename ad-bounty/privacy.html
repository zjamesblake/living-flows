Privacy Policy

Ad Bounty System — Meta App

About this policy

This privacy policy describes how the Ad Bounty System Meta app (the “App”) collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when operated by Easy A Media (“we”, “us”, the “Operator”) on behalf of partner brands.

The App is an internal-use, server-to-server integration that operates the Ad Bounty creator program. It is not consumer-facing and does not present a login surface to end users. It accesses Meta Marketing API data on behalf of partner brands that have explicitly granted access to the Operator’s Business Manager via Meta Business Manager partner sharing.

Who we are

Operator: Easy A Media (Meta Business Manager: Media A Easy, ID 152562646477468)
Contact: zak@reversescaling.com

What information the App accesses

The App accesses the following data from Meta on behalf of partner brand ad accounts that have explicitly shared access with the Operator:

  • Ad account metadata: account ID, name, currency, status, business owner.
  • Ads, ad creatives, ad sets, campaigns: IDs, names, statuses, configuration.
  • Creative assets: video IDs, image hashes, and the creative files uploaded by the App.
  • Insights data: aggregate performance metrics (spend, impressions, clicks, conversions) at the ad level.
  • Page metadata: Page ID and name where ads are posted “as” the brand Page.

The App does not collect personal data from end users of the partner brands’ ads (consumers who see or click the ads). The App does not collect data from individuals beyond what is necessary to operate the partner brand’s Meta advertising assets.

How we use the information

Data accessed via the Meta Marketing API is used solely to:

  • Upload approved creative assets from the Ad Bounty creator program into the partner brand’s Meta ad account.
  • Associate creator submissions with the resulting Meta ad objects (ad IDs, creative IDs) for performance reporting.
  • Read aggregate ad performance to calculate creator payouts and produce performance reports for the partner brand.
  • Maintain operational records for audit, debugging, and reconciliation.

We do not sell, rent, or share partner brand data with third parties. We do not use partner brand data for targeting, profiling, or any purpose outside the operation of the Ad Bounty program for that brand.

How we store and retain information

Information accessed via Meta is stored in a private Supabase Postgres database operated by Easy A Media. Access is restricted to authorized Operator staff and the App’s server-side processes. Data is retained for the duration of the partner brand’s engagement with the Ad Bounty program, plus a reasonable archival period for financial reconciliation (typically 12–24 months after engagement end).

Partner brands may request deletion of their stored data at any time by contacting us at the address above.

How we share information

We share data only with:

  • Meta Platforms, via the Marketing API, in the normal course of operating the partner brand’s ad account.
  • Sub-processors we use to operate the App: Supabase (database hosting), Railway (application hosting), Google (Drive API for creative file access).
  • The partner brand themselves, when delivering operational reports.

We do not share data with any other party without the partner brand’s explicit instruction.

Partner brand rights

Partner brands whose Meta assets the App accesses may at any time:

  • Revoke the App’s access via their own Meta Business Manager (Business Settings → Ad Accounts → Assigned Partners).
  • Request a copy of data we hold relating to their ad account by emailing us.
  • Request deletion of their data from our systems by emailing us.

Security

Access tokens, service-account credentials, and database connection details are stored as encrypted environment variables in our hosting infrastructure. The App uses Meta System User tokens and signs requests with appsecret_proof to verify server-side origin. We follow standard practices for rotating credentials and limiting access on a least-privilege basis.

Children

The App is a B2B operational tool and is not intended for use by individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
