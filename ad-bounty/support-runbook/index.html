Ad Bounty — Support Runbook

When a creator opens a ticket → what to do. Click any row to expand.
student self-serve (bot) support action (dashboard) admin / escalate to Zak automatic / manual
Support: Astrid, Karlo, Lovro  ·  Admin: Zak, Ghed  ·  Dashboard: ad-bounty-dashboard.vercel.app (open the creator's profile)  ·  Bot commands: creator types them in #bounty-bot

1 · Student / Creator

"I never got my welcome message / any DMs"DMs disabled, or unreachable▶
DASHBOARD Open their profile → check the reachability badge.
If unreachable: have them turn on DMs (Server → Privacy Settings → allow DMs), then click Re-send Welcome.
If they still can't receive DMs: tell them to run /me and /status in #bounty-bot for everything — the bot is their fallback.
"My WHOP lapsed — I fixed my payment but I'm still locked out"can't submit▶
DASHBOARD Click Refresh WHOP on their profile — re-checks WHOP instantly and restores them. No waiting for the nightly job.
"My name / country is wrong"▶
DASHBOARD Edit Profile — support can fix name, Discord username, country, and notes.
"My WHOP email / Discord ID is wrong"load-bearing keys▶
ESCALATE These are locked to admins (a wrong value silently breaks WHOP checks or DM routing). Hand to Zak / Ghed.
"I need to update my payout method"▶
BOT Tell them to run /payment-info in #bounty-bot — fully self-serve.
DASHBOARD Or edit their payment fields on the profile.
"Am I approved? What's my status?"▶
BOT /me — shows their status, Creator ID, and brands.

2 · Enrollment

"How do I join a brand?"▶
BOT /me — lists available brands with enrollment links.
"I never got my enrollment confirmation"▶
DASHBOARD Profile → Enrollment → Re-send Confirmation for that brand.
"I didn't get / I lost my dashboard link"▶
BOT Fastest: tell them to run /me — it shows their dashboard link per brand.
DASHBOARD Or Enrollment → Re-send Dashboard.
ESCALATE If no dashboard link exists yet (setup never ran), hand to Zak.
"Where's my submission link?"▶
BOT /me — shows the submit link for each enrolled brand.

3 · Submission & QA

"Where's my ad? I haven't heard back"▶
BOT /status — shows every active submission, its stage, and time-in-stage. Accurate and up to date.
"I didn't get my revision request / I don't understand it"▶
DASHBOARD Submissions → Re-send Revision to re-deliver the feedback + resubmit link.
BOT Or /revisions shows open revisions with the failed gates.
MANUAL If the feedback itself is unclear, that's a support-ticket conversation (no self-serve path).
"Where do I resubmit?"▶
BOT /revisions or /status — the resubmit link is inline.
"Did my resubmission go through?"▶
AUTOMATIC They receive a resubmission confirmation DM. Also visible via /status.
"Was my ad approved? Is it live?"▶
BOT /status shows ready to launch / launched — reliable now that the matcher runs. That's everything they need.
Note: we don't fire a DM on launch today (parked — nice-to-have, not pressing).
"My submission has been stuck for days"▶
HANDLED Rare — Zak runs a daily queue sweep, so nothing sits in QA longer than ~a day. Reassure them; if it's genuinely stuck past that, flag Zak.

4 · Payout

"My payment info is wrong / needs updating"▶
BOT /payment-info — self-serve view + update (audited).
DASHBOARD Or support edits payment fields on the profile.
"I wasn't paid / I'm missing a payout"▶
HANDLED Reassure: failed payments are automatically detected and re-sent in a mid-month sweep (~10 days after the payout run). Have them confirm their payout method is correct via /payment-info.
ESCALATE If it's still missing after that sweep, hand to Zak.
"My payout amount looks wrong"▶
ESCALATE Hand to Zak — adjustments run through the payout-adjust process (admin only).
"When will I get paid?"▶
INFO Spend is pulled around the 3rd of the month; payments go out over the following week. Creators receive a DM when their payment is being processed.

5 · Brand

"A brand I'm in went away" / "Can I join a new brand?"▶
BOT /me always reflects their current + available brands.
Brand open/close announcements are handled by Zak (brands rarely toggle).
"How do I contact a brand directly?"▶
MANUAL No in-system channel. Brand owners communicate requirements in the community channels; point creators there.

✓ Already handled (not gaps)

  • Resubmission confirmation — creators do get a DM when a resubmission lands.
  • "Is my ad live?" — the matcher keeps /status accurate; that covers it.
  • Stuck submissions — the daily queue sweep clears QA within ~a day.
  • Failed payments — the mid-month sweep auto-detects bounces and re-sends.

~ Accepted limitations (works, just not self-serve)

  • Payout history — no student self-serve view; support checks the dashboard, and creators get a processing DM.
  • Revision follow-up questions — handled via support ticket.
  • Brand contact — via the community channels, not an in-system channel.

◔ Parked ideas (nice-to-have, not now)

  • "Your ad launched" DM — proactive launch notification.
  • Student-facing QA guide — plain-language explanation of the gate criteria.
